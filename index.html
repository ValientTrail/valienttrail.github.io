About Me

Passionate Game Developer

My name is Raymond Zegles, a game programmer and pixel artist focused on crafting engaging game mechanics. My journey into gaming began at 13 with Command & Conquer Generals: Zero Hour and Star Wars: Empire at War: Forces of Corruption, old but amazing games that sparked my passion for gaming. I got into game development in college and have since built up experience with Unity, Unreal Engine 5, and pixel art creation in Aseprite. With a solid foundation in C++ and C#, and currently expanding my skills in Python, I bring both technical expertise and collaborative energy to team projects. Whether programming systems or creating pixel art assets, I'm driven by the challenge of bringing gameplay ideas to life.

MONSTER!

Completed
